Ideation

Design Requirements

Once we recognized the pain points and desires of our users, we began developing upon the concrete design needs of our product. We listed out seven different realistic design requirements we wanted our product to implement:

  • Allow users to easily access information about paintings and artists with a portable device
  • Allow users to physically interact with the art
  • Allow users to easily navigate the museum to find specific art pieces and exhibits
  • Allow the product to be accessible and usable by people of all abilities and backgrounds
  • Allow users to upload comments or reflections about artwork
  • Allow users to see their path and time spent throughout the museum
  • Allow users to be directed towards other similar pieces in a collection or area of the museum

Takeaways

These requirements helped us focus our scope and realize specific design goals for our project.

Storyboards

Using the scenarios and problem statement, we created storyboards following users experiencing a problem faced throughout the museum visit and a solution in the form of our product that could help them achieve their end goals.
